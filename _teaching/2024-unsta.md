I currently serve as an **Associate Professor** for the course **Economics for Engineers**, a position I have held since **October 2024**.

### Course Details:

* **Subject:** Economics for Engineers
* **Role:** Associate Professor
* **Key Topics:**
    * **Introduction to Microeconomics:** Supply and demand, market structures, and pricing.
    * **Introduction to Macroeconomics:** National accounts, inflation, and fiscal policy.
    * **Investment Project Formulation:** Financial evaluation and project feasibility analysis.
