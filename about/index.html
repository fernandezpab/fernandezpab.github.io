About Me

Hi there! My name is Pablo Fernandez. I am from San Miguel of Tucuman, Argentina. Some of my favorite things to do are read, play the piano, and swimming. These are activities that I enjoy immensely and indulge in daily. My career aspirations are to become an active member of society through either a civil or transportation engineer. I have been told that I am a charming, kind, attitude-prone person with "oddities". I look up mostly to my family members, especially my mom, as a guiding figure in life. Favorite shows of mine include Doctor Who, Sherlock, Triple D (Diners, Drive-Ins' and Dives), Hustle, and Chopped (yes, the majority of those are British TV shows, problem, mate?).
